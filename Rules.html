Hostel Rule Book

Introduction

Welcome to the CCET Boys Hostel. In order to maintain a safe, comfortable, and harmonious living environment for all residents, we have established the following rules and regulations. Please familiarize yourself with these rules and adhere to them during your stay.

Rules and Regulations

  1. No unauthorized guests allowed in the hostel premises.
  2. Quiet hours are enforced from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. Please respect your fellow residents' need for rest and study during these hours.
  3. Smoking, drinking alcohol, and using drugs are strictly prohibited within the hostel premises.
  4. All residents must keep their rooms and common areas clean and tidy at all times.
  5. Visitors must register at the reception and leave their ID cards with the security guard during their stay.
  6. Individuals with One Dose of Covid-19 Vaccination and negative report of RT-PCR in last 72 hours are allowed in hostels.

Disciplinary Action

Violation of any of the hostel rules may result in disciplinary action, including warnings, fines, or expulsion from the hostel. The severity of the action will depend on the nature and frequency of the violation.
